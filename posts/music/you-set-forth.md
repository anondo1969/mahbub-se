তোমার হাতে রয়, আমার হাতে ক্ষয়--

You set forth as I fade away,
You and I together, as rivers sway.

Your lamp glows, a partner in your space.
While night is for me, stars for my pace.

The shore is for you; I have the open seas.
You reflect in time, I march with ease.

Your hands safeguard, mine lay waste,
Your heart harbors fear, mine, a fearless quest.
