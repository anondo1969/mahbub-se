# Notes from a first job

This is an older piece, written years ago and kept here in the archive of early writing. The early-writings stream is deliberately separate from current notes: same renderer, different shelf.

I remember thinking that shipping software was the hard part. It turned out the hard part was everything around it: the people, the patience, the slow accrual of trust.

> Old writing is a photograph of who you were. You do not edit a photograph.

I leave these as they were, typos of thought and all.
