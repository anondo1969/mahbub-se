# 13th International Conference on Health Informatics (HEALTHINF 2020) and the 'best paper award', 24-26 February, 2020

I presented a research paper entitled '[Deep Learning from Heterogeneous Sequences of Sparse Medical Data for Early Prediction of Sepsis](http://www.scitepress.org/DigitalLibrary/Link.aspx?doi=10.5220/0008911400450055)' in the 13th International Conference on Health Informatics (HEALTHINF), Malta, Valletta, 24-26 February 2020 organized by the Institute for Systems and Technologies of Information, Control and Communication (INSTICC). The paper won the [HEALTHINF 2020 Best Paper Award](https://healthinf.scitevents.org/PreviousAwards.aspx#2020). Overall it was an enjoyable experience, and I learned a lot from all.
